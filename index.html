Some high-impact responsibilities you will be entrusted with:
  • Understanding the Claim RI process as well as documentation requirements from all insurance companies.
  • Reviewing documents submitted by clients and following up with customers to ensure they comply with necessary specifications. Escalation to account management team when necessary.
  • Submitting claims to insurers and following up with insurers for case resolution, while escalating to the insurer and Internal Management whenever necessary in order to close case within Insurer SLA.
  • Record and maintain accurate information of all ongoing claims in our CRM system – Salesforce
  • Monitoring the Claim Dashboard on daily basis to ensure the handling of customer requests within set KPIs.
  • Provide feedback, suggestions and support for the continuous improvement of the claim experience/process.
  • Turnaround time for claim placement, indemnification, insurer follow up and escalation
  • # of Claims and Pre-Approvals processed on average per day/week
  • Customer Satisfaction survey of Claims & Preauths overturned monthly Exception & Escalation of claims handled monthly (on average of 260)
What you will need to have:
  • 2-3 years and above of experience in health insurance industry like hospitals, insurance companies, TPAs.
  • Any kind of Medical degree holder 
  • Well organized detail-oriented & customer centric.
  • Strong verbal and written communications skills .
